# Minimum Foundation Platform (MFP) v1.1

## Purpose

The Minimum Foundation Platform (MFP) defines the smallest implementation of VentureOS capable of proving its core philosophy.

The purpose of the MFP is to demonstrate that a governed, evidence-led, object-centric approach produces better venture decisions than a traditional AI chat workflow.

Every capability included in the MFP must directly support that objective.

Everything else is deferred.

## Success Definition

The MFP is successful when a single user can investigate a venture from initial idea through to a fully auditable Board decision.

The complete reasoning process must remain permanently traceable.

Core Demonstration

Starting with only a venture idea, VentureOS should assist the user in producing an evidence-backed Board decision.

Every observation, assumption, hypothesis, challenge, and decision should remain permanently linked and reviewable.

Primary User

The initial user is the Founding Board.

Multi-user collaboration is intentionally deferred.

Authentication should remain minimal.

The objective is organisational reasoning rather than user management.

Core Capabilities
Case Management

The user can:

create Cases
update Case details
archive Cases
reopen Cases
browse Cases
Venture Research

The system can:

investigate a venture opportunity
identify observations
identify evidence
identify assumptions
identify hypotheses
identify risks
identify validation activities
preserve research provenance
assess research quality
require human review before acceptance

Research should investigate the venture using a consistent framework rather than simply answering prompts.

Observation Management

The user can:

create observations
accept AI-generated observations
import observations from documents
import observations from websites
tag observations
associate observations with Cases
Evidence Management

The user can:

create evidence
support observations
contradict observations
associate evidence with hypotheses
record confidence
preserve provenance
Hypothesis Management

The user can:

create assumptions
create hypotheses
create opportunities
revise hypotheses
associate supporting evidence
change lifecycle status
Research Quality

The system can:

validate AI-generated research
identify unsupported claims
identify missing information
identify structural issues
preserve quality findings for human review

Quality assessment informs human judgement.

It does not replace it.

Red Team

The system can:

challenge assumptions
identify contradictory evidence
expose weak reasoning
identify missing validation
propose additional investigation

The objective is constructive challenge.

Board Review

The system prepares a Board briefing containing:

executive summary
observations
evidence
assumptions
hypotheses
objections
risks
confidence
quality findings
recommended investigations

The Board remains responsible for every decision.

Decision Recording

The Board can:

approve
reject
defer
request further investigation

Every decision records:

rationale
supporting evidence
outstanding uncertainty
Timeline

Every significant event automatically becomes part of the Case timeline.

Examples include:

research completed
observation accepted
evidence created
hypothesis revised
challenge raised
Board decision
lesson recorded

The timeline forms the audit trail.

Lessons

The Board can record lessons.

Lessons remain permanently associated with their originating Case.

Future Cases should be able to reference previous lessons.

Organisational Roles

The MFP supports three organisational roles.

Research

Investigates opportunities.

Produces structured proposals.

Red Team

Challenges reasoning.

Requests additional investigation.

Identifies weaknesses.

Board

Reviews evidence.

Makes decisions.

Records rationale.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is used where it demonstrably improves organisational capability.

Examples include:

venture research
observation extraction
evidence identification
summarisation
classification
Red Team analysis
Board briefing preparation

AI proposes.

Humans review.

The Domain remains authoritative.

Local First

The preferred deployment model is local.

Preferred technologies include:

local language models
local embeddings
local vector search
local database
open-source tooling

Cloud services remain optional.

Individual AI capabilities may use external providers where they provide materially better outcomes.

The architecture should remain provider-agnostic.

autonomous deployment

Definition of Done

The MFP is complete when the following demonstration can be performed.

A user creates a new Case.

The system performs a structured venture investigation.

Research proposals undergo quality assessment.

The user accepts observations, evidence, assumptions, and hypotheses.

The Red Team challenges the reasoning.

Additional investigation is performed where required.

The Board receives a structured briefing.

A decision is recorded.

The reasoning remains permanently auditable.

Weeks later, a user can understand:

what was known
what remained uncertain
what assumptions existed
what evidence supported the decision
what objections were raised
why the Board decided as it did
what ultimately happened
Success Metrics

The MFP succeeds if it demonstrates:

evidence-led reasoning
governed AI
complete traceability
transparent decision making
persistent organisational knowledge
repeatable venture investigation

Success is measured by the quality of organisational decision making.

Guiding Principle

Whenever implementation choices become difficult, choose the option that best supports the mission.

Turn uncertainty into understanding.

Turn understanding into evidence.

Turn evidence into decisions.

Turn decisions into successful ventures.

Implementation Boundary

Version 0.1 is complete when the MFP demonstrates the VentureOS philosophy end-to-end.

Features that do not strengthen that demonstration are intentionally deferred.
